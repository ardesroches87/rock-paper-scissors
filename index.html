Rock ○ Paper ○ Scissors

How to play

  • Click on one of the hand positions: rock, paper, or scissors.
  • The computer will randomly choose one of the hand positions at the same time.
  • If you both choose the same position, it will be a tie.
  • If you choose different hand positions, your choices and points will be displayed below.
  • Whoever gets up to 5 points first is the winner of the round!
